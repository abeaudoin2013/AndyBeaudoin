Vicarious.

Using Google Maps street view API, I was able to create a system in which users can make posts at specific latitudes and longitudes. The posts can be linked together by one story. The idea was to make an interactive travel blog, so that you could get a better picture of what your friends' trips around the world were like.

Block

This app was designed for writers and creatives struggling with writer's block. With the help of my team we created three different kinds of writing exercises that are randomly generated. Users are meant to write at least 250 words within ten minutes. In the future we hope to add another main feature which allows writers to store their documents on the web so that other writers can pull copies down to edit and critique each other's work.

Some Are Born

Some Are Born is a feature-length independent film written by Bill Vincent (a prestigious scriptwriter and professor who helped create the original Evil Dead). 95% of the film was shot within one month in various locations in Michigan. Along with my partner, Josh Buscher, we designed the aesthetic look of the characters, their props, their environment. We even got a chance to work with Betsy Baker, an original cast member from Evil Dead.
